Plain prose is the baseline: a calm measure, a comfortable line-height, a soft contrast between ink and page. Within it, words earn emphasis only when they must — **bold** for weight, *italic* for voice, ***both together*** for the rare case where one reinforces the other. Links carry two jobs. An internal link points at somewhere on this site and should feel continuous with the prose, while an external link hands the reader off to another world entirely. Hover them and the underline should firm up without the color doing anything dramatic.
